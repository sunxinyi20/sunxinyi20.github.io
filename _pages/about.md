I am a PhD Candidate in Quantitative Marketing at the **Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST)**, advised by Professor [Jia Liu](https://bm.hkust.edu.hk/people/faculty/detail/jialiu). My research examines **how AI and digital platforms reshape brand building, consumer behavior, and content strategy** — developing AI-native measurement tools and empirical frameworks to answer questions that traditional methods cannot.

My work spans three interconnected themes: (1) **Digital brand building and content strategy** — how durable brand equity is built in content-driven digital markets; (2) **AI-mediated consumer behavior** — how AI-powered tools such as LLM chatbots reshape consumer search and decision-making; and (3) **AI tools for marketing research** — how generative AI (LLMs and diffusion models) can be systematically integrated into research methods and industry practice.

Methodologically, I draw on **causal inference** (RDiT, field experiments), **large language models** (multi-agent architectures, prompt engineering), and **computer vision** (Stable Diffusion, CLIP, LoRA).

I am on the 2026–2027 academic job market.
